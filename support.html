Permissions

ColorPlate may request access to your camera, photo library, or location depending on the feature you choose. You can change these permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

Analysis results

ColorPlate's color and ingredient analysis is intended for visual memory and exploration. It may be imperfect and should not be used for allergies, medical decisions, food safety, diagnosis, or nutrition planning.
