Privacy

Privacy Policy.

Last updated: May 2026

ColorPlate is built around your personal color archive. This page explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Summary

ColorPlate does not require an account. Your archive is designed to stay on your device. The app uses selected meal photos to create color and ingredient analysis, and it uses optional permissions only when you choose features that need them.

No account required

ColorPlate does not require an account to create your archive. Your meal archive, analysis results, and color history are stored locally on your device.

Information processed by the app

  • Meal photos you choose from your library or capture with the camera.
  • Color and ingredient analysis generated from those photos.
  • Meal dates, meal type, saved notes, streaks, scores, and archive artwork generated in the app.
  • Optional location only when you request nearby food discovery.
  • Optional support emails or privacy requests you send to us.

Photos and analysis

When you choose or capture a meal photo, ColorPlate uses it to identify colors and ingredients and create your analysis. Photos are used for this purpose only. ColorPlate does not use your meal photos for advertising or tracking.

Optional services

Some analysis may be performed with secure third-party AI providers. If a photo is sent to a provider, it is sent only to generate the requested ColorPlate analysis. If you connect your own AI key, it is stored in the iOS keychain and is not transmitted to ColorPlate servers.

Location

ColorPlate only uses your location when you request nearby food recommendations from the Eat view. Location access is optional and is not used silently in the background.

Data retention

Your archive remains on your device until you delete entries or remove the app. Support emails and privacy requests are kept only as long as needed to respond, maintain records, and meet legal obligations.

Your control

You can delete entries in the app, delete the app to remove locally stored data, and manage camera, photo library, and location permissions in iOS Settings. Since the archive is local, you remain in control of your stored meal history.

Tracking and advertising

ColorPlate does not sell your data and does not use meal photos or archive history for third-party advertising. If analytics or crash reporting are added in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Children

ColorPlate is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can help.

Changes

We may update this policy when ColorPlate changes. The current version will be posted here with the latest update date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact privacy@colorplate.app. For app support, contact support@colorplate.app.
