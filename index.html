App Store information

Clear, review-ready basics.

01

Data and privacy

No account is required. Meal photos are used to create the analysis and archive. Location is optional and used only for nearby food discovery.

02

AI processing

Some analysis may use secure third-party AI providers. Users are told when a selected photo is processed for color and ingredient analysis.

03

Regulatory status

ColorPlate is a visual food archive. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, dietary, or clinical advice.

04

Support

Support, privacy requests, and legal contact are available from this site and inside the app settings.
